Who was King George III?

Mad George or George the three was born on the 4th June 1738 in London. He was the eldest son of Frederick, Prince of Wales and Princess Augusta of Saxe-gotha. He is better known as the king who lost the American colonies and for losing his mind. He became king of Great Britain and Ireland year 1760 after his grandfather’s death at the age of 22. He was quite isolated by his mother and tutored by a Scottish nobleman named Lard Bute. King George was the first British monarch to have an interest and regularly studied science.
